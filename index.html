Rules for Bulls and Cows

  • Step 1: The computer generates random 5 digit number with unique values
  • Step 2: You enter 5 digit number to try and guess the computer number
  • Step 3: If you have a digit matching the exact position of the number to be guessed you receive a bull. If you have guessed a digit, but not on the correct possition, you receive a cow
  • Step 4: Combine numbers until you manage to guess the computer's number
